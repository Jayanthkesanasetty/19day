ABOUT US

Founder promoter members of the Society, who are Primarily Businessmen, Believe that the future of India can be built and moulded in class rooms; the total education can play a very important role for peaceful and co-operative existence of all human beings.The National and International understanding can be Built-up only through the Tool of Education, thereby after careful and meaningful discussions among ourselves, We desired to establish a society by the name " KALLAM ACADEMY OF EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY ". The Society is registered as vide Reg. No. 24 of 2006 dated 17-01-2006 under the Andhra Pradesh Societies Registration Act, 35 of 2001.

Science and Technology have emerged major factors in the sustenance of the society.They are changing the world with a matchless speed and magnitude.In this scenario , to create competent technocrats, an ideal institution with good ambience, infrastructure and eminent faculty is required.

With an objective to create technically skillful professionals and to extend the legacy of success in industry to the arena of education, KALLAM GROUP OF INDUSTRIES chairman Sri KALLAM HARANADHA REDDY under the aegis of KALLAM ACADEMY OF EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY established the "KALLAM HARANADHAREDDY INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY" in 2010 at chowdavaram. The college provides a lush green and peaceful environment which is located at about 10 kms away from guntur towards chilakaluripet on NH-5.

KALLAM HARANADHAREDDY INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY always stands forefront in equipping students with distinct academic and social skills preparing them to stand out among their peers.The founder's visionary, industrial and philanthropic tenets gave new meaning to the way quality higher education and research provided.

KALLAM HARANADHAREDDY INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY of its infrastructure including well equipped departmental blocks, lecture halls, seminar rooms , the central library , state of the art laboratories , workshops , hostels , dining halls and sports facilities.

PLACEMENT CELL

The Training & Placement Cell at KHIT is a very strong department with an impeccable track record in placing students right after their graduation. The Training & Placement Cell constantly keeps in touch with HRs of various major MNCs and IT companies to enhance the employability of the students.

KHIT is proud to share that our college got MOUs with more than 15 International and National companies for providing competency based placements, internship training, faculty development program and industrial visits etc. In addition, we achieved 100% placements by providing to all eligible engineering graduates of our 1st outgoing batch of 2010-14. In order to give students an opportunity to apply their knowledge and skills the campus has been maintaining close liaison with the reputed companies throughout the country for the final placement. In addition, we are conducting training programs for GRE; TOFEL & GATE regularly to the final and pre-final students of all classes along with CRT (campus Recruitment Training) to impart competitive skills, communication skills, mock interview and group discussion techniques. For functioning of TPO Cell, a senior professor is acting as an Training and Placement Officer and he is assisted by placement associates from different departments.
